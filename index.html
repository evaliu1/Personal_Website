About Me


Engineering isn't about coding programs and wiring electronics. Engineering is to design, to create, to provide solutions for a better life. It is to think beyond the expectation and define the future. That is why I look further for every project I did, think the connections between technology and application, propose different approaches and optimize my work by thousands of testing.

I believe self-learning is the key to succeeding. I'm always keen to obtain new knowledge and get myself on top of required skills in a short time. Moreover, I tend to learn more about what I am interested, combine the knowledge from different related areas and complete projects to practice new learning. All in all, I'm always motivated to explore and create the world using my intelligence.
